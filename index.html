CIVIL SERVICE EXAM PRETEST 1

Created by: CSE Learning Essentials

Section 4: General Information - Part 4: Environment Management and Protection

Passing Score: 8/10

Directions: Read each question carefully and choose the correct answer from the choices given.

1. Which law is known as the “Ecological Solid Waste Management Act of 2000”?

  • A) R.A. 8749
  • B) R.A. 9003
  • C) R.A. 9275
  • D) R.A. 9512
  • E) R.A. 9729

2. The “Philippine Clean Water Act of 2004” is also known as:

  • A) R.A. 8749
  • B) R.A. 9275
  • C) R.A. 7586
  • D) R.A. 9003
  • E) R.A. 6969

3. The Climate Change Commission (CCC) was created under what law?

  • A) R.A. 8749
  • B) R.A. 9275
  • C) R.A. 9729
  • D) R.A. 9003
  • E) R.A. 9512

4. Republic Act No. 6969 is otherwise known as the:

  • A) Philippine Mining Act of 1995
  • B) Toxic Substances and Hazardous and Nuclear Wastes Control Act of 1990
  • C) Philippine Fisheries Code of 1998
  • D) National Integrated Protected Areas System (NIPAS) Act of 1992
  • E) Renewable Energy Act of 2008

5. Which government agency is tasked to enforce the Ecological Solid Waste Management Act (R.A. 9003)?

  • A) Department of Health (DOH)
  • B) Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG)
  • C) Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR)
  • D) Department of Agriculture (DA)
  • E) Department of Energy (DOE)

6. Which environmental law focuses primarily on the management of hazardous and nuclear wastes?

  • A) R.A. 8749
  • B) R.A. 9003
  • C) R.A. 9275
  • D) R.A. 6969
  • E) R.A. 9729

7. What is the primary aim of Republic Act No. 7586, also known as the NIPAS Act?

  • A) To regulate toxic substances
  • B) To promote the use of renewable energy
  • C) To establish a comprehensive system of protected areas in the Philippines
  • D) To regulate water and air pollution
  • E) To create a climate change framework

8. Under R.A. 9003, segregation of wastes shall primarily be conducted at the:

  • A) Barangay Materials Recovery Facility
  • B) Dumpsite
  • C) Household level
  • D) School campus
  • E) City landfill

9. Which among the following is considered a renewable source of energy?

  • A) Coal
  • B) Petroleum
  • C) Wind energy
  • D) Natural gas
  • E) Uranium

10. What is the main purpose of R.A. 9512, also known as the Environmental Awareness and Education Act of 2008?

  • A) To penalize illegal mining
  • B) To integrate environmental education in school curricula
  • C) To promote tree planting in urban areas
  • D) To regulate hazardous waste disposal
  • E) To ban the use of ozone-depleting substances
